Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Nephy, your financial privacy is our highest priority. This policy outlines how Nephy handles your data. To sum it up: we do not collect, store, or sell any of your personal financial information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Because Nephy operates serverless, we do not require you to create an account or sign up to use the app. As a developer, I do not have access to, collect, or store any of the following details:

  • Your income, budgets, expenses, or transactions.
  • Your custom categories, keywords, or tags.
  • Your partner's name, email, or sharing details.

2. How Your Data is Stored

Nephy uses Apple's native database technologies to store and synchronize your budget data securely:

  • Local Storage: All budgets, categories, and expenses are saved directly on your iOS device via local database frameworks (SwiftData).
  • iCloud Sync: If iCloud is enabled on your device, your private data is securely backed up and synchronized across your own Apple devices using your private iCloud container.
  • Partner Sharing: When you share costs using the "Couple" category, synchronization is handled directly between your Apple iCloud container and your partner's iCloud container. This data is fully encrypted end-to-end.

Only you and your partner can access your data. The developer has no keys or means to access your iCloud storage.

3. Third-Party Services & Analytics

Nephy does not contain any advertising SDKs, third-party analytics libraries, or external trackers. The app runs completely within Apple's secure ecosystem and relies exclusively on Apple's standard operating system APIs.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page and the app. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of these terms.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data security, please feel free to reach out to the developer at ocher_temp2l@icloud.com.
