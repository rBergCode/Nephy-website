Setting Up Nephy in 4 Steps

Get started quickly and experience visual finance sync built right into Apple's ecosystem.

1

Add Categories & Keywords

Create your custom categories (Groceries, Dining, Transport) and assign keyword triggers to detect merchants automatically.

2

Set Private Budgets

Define your spending limits per category. Only your individual share will calculate towards these limits, leaving you in total control.

3

Mark & Sync Couple Costs

As expenses happen, tap 'Couple' for shared bills. Nephy automatically divides the expense and syncs the records securely with your partner via iCloud.

4

Watch Your Weather

Open the dashboard to review your financial health. Sunny weather lets you know you are safe to spend, while showers suggest caution.
